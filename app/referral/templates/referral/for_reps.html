The gift

How the thank-you gift works.

Costs you nothing. Comes from the dealership marketing budget.

The dealership pays
Comes from the dealer's marketing budget — same place they'd pay for a Facebook ad or radio spot. Not from your commission.
The dealer sets the gift
Each dealer decides their own thank-you gift amount based on what works for their business. Bird Dogs see "a thank-you gift" — actual amount confirmed when the deal closes.
Sent 7–14 days after sale
Once you mark the deal closed, the dealership sends the Bird Dog their gift — check, gift card, or store credit. The Bird Dog gets a notification automatically.
Zero paperwork for you
No 1099. No tax forms on your end. No commission split. The system tracks everything; your job is closing deals.
Real-world tips

What the top reps do.

A few things that separate the reps who build a real Bird Dog Network from the ones who try it once and forget.

  • 1
    Be consistent. Ask every customer you'd be happy to do business with again — not just the ones you think will say yes. You're not selling them; you're handing them a tool.
  • 2
    Use their phone, not yours. They scan the QR or you text them the link. Signup happens on their device so the account is theirs and they have it forever.
  • 3
    Soft sell only. "If you ever know someone..." not "I need referrals." It's an offer, not an ask. The thank-you gift does the selling for you.
  • 4
    Follow up after 30 days. Text your Bird Dogs: "Hey, anyone in your circle car shopping?" Gentle reminder that they have your link. Builds the muscle.
  • 5
    Compounds over time. A rep with 20 active Bird Dogs gets a steady drip of referrals every month. The customer you sign up today might send three buyers in the next year.
Common questions

The stuff every rep asks before trying it.

Will these referrals compete with my dealership leads?
No. Bird Dog referrals come back to YOU specifically — tagged to your name, on your storefront, in your CRM. They never enter the general dealership lead pool. This is additive to whatever the dealership sends you, not a replacement.
Do I have to track anything manually?
No. Your dashboard shows everything automatically — who's signed up as a Bird Dog under you, what referrals have come in, what's pending, what's closed, and what gifts have been sent. You just close deals like normal.
What if my customers say no?
Most won't sign up — and that's fine. The math works on volume. If you invite 10 customers and 3 sign up, those 3 might send 1-2 friends each over the next year. That's real, additive lead flow you didn't have before.
How quickly will I see results?
First Bird Dog signups happen the day you start asking. First referrals usually arrive within 30 days. First closed deal from a Bird Dog referral typically lands in month 2-3. The system compounds — month 6 is way more than month 1.
What if I leave the dealership?
Your Bird Dogs are tied to you, not to the dealership. If you move, you take your network with you. (Specifics depend on your employment agreement — but the system is designed around rep-owned relationships, not dealership-owned ones.)
Is this just for senior reps?
No — works for any rep at any tenure. Senior reps have an advantage because they have more past customers to invite, but a new rep building their book can use this to grow faster than they would otherwise.
