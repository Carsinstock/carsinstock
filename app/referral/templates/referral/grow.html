Step one

5 ways to share your link.

Pick the one that fits your style. Most Bird Dogs use 2 or 3 of these regularly.

Text it directly
When someone in your phone mentions car shopping — even in passing — send them your link with a short note. The most natural and the highest converting.
Post on Facebook
A weekly post — "If you're car shopping, hit me up. I can connect you with someone I trust" — keeps your link in front of your full network without being pushy.
Drop in group chats
Work group chats, family threads, neighborhood groups — anywhere people might mention car trouble or shopping. Quiet hand-raise, not a sales pitch.
Show your QR in person
Your dashboard has a QR code — pull it up when you're at work or socializing. People can scan it in seconds. Great for service-business owners (hair, nails, mechanics, real estate).
Add to your email signature
A single line at the bottom: "Know anyone car shopping? Send them my way → mycarreferral.com/your-slug." Passive, persistent, costs you zero effort.
Share with a story
When you next buy or service a car, share the experience and add your link. Real stories convert better than cold pitches every single time.

What to say in person.

Real talk

What makes a top Bird Dog.

The folks who actually earn aren't doing anything magical. They're doing a few small things consistently.

  • 1
    Be patient. Most car buyers take 30-90 days to actually buy. Don't worry if your dashboard is empty in week one — it's normal.
  • 2
    Quality over quantity. Sending your link to 5 people who are actually shopping beats blasting it to 500 who aren't. Pay attention to who's mentioned cars lately.
  • 3
    Follow up casually. A week or two after you send your link: "Hey, did you get a chance to check out that rep I told you about?" Gentle nudge. No pressure.
  • 4
    Don't oversell. You're not a salesperson — you're a connector. The rep does the selling. Your job is just to make the introduction.
  • 5
    Build the habit. Mention it in one conversation a week. Post on Facebook once a month. Add it to your email signature. Small, consistent inputs compound fast.
Questions

What everyone wants to know by week two.

When do I get my thank-you gift?
Usually 7–14 days after the deal closes. The dealership handles it — they'll send a check, gift card, or store credit depending on their setup. You'll see the gift confirmation in your dashboard the moment they mark it sent.
What if my friend doesn't buy?
No deal, no gift — but no cost or hassle for you either. The system only pays when an actual sale happens. You don't owe anyone anything, and they don't owe you anything.
How long does my referral stay attached to me?
90 days from the first click. So if your friend takes a few weeks to think it over, you still get credit when they buy. Just make sure they use your link the first time they reach out.
How much will the gift be?
Each dealership sets their own thank-you gift amount based on what works for their business. The exact amount is confirmed when the deal closes — you'll see it in your dashboard and in the confirmation email.
Can I refer more than one person?
Absolutely. There's no limit. Every closed deal earns its own thank-you gift. Some Bird Dogs send 2-3 referrals a year; some send 20. It's your network, your pace.
What if I want to stop?
Easy. Just stop sharing your link. Your account stays open in case you change your mind, but nothing happens unless you actively share. No fees, no obligations, no contracts.
