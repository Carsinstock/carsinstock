Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Introduction

CarsInStock ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website carsinstock.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account and use our platform:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password
  • Profile information: display name, phone number, bio, and profile photos
  • Vehicle listings: make, model, year, price, VIN, photos, and descriptions
  • Lead and customer data: names, email addresses, phone numbers, and messages submitted through storefronts
  • Communications: messages sent through our contact form or chatbot

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our site, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data via Google Analytics (GA4), including pages visited, time on site, referral sources, and device information
  • Session data through cookies necessary for the platform to function
  • Security verification data through Cloudflare Turnstile to prevent spam and abuse

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the CarsInStock platform
  • Create and manage your salesperson storefront
  • Deliver leads and customer inquiries to you
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets, lead notifications)
  • Process payments for your subscription
  • Analyze site usage to improve the user experience
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the platform:

  • SendGrid — for sending transactional and notification emails
  • Cloudinary — for hosting and delivering vehicle and profile images
  • Cloudflare — for security, performance, and CAPTCHA verification
  • Google Analytics — for understanding site traffic and usage patterns
  • Stripe — for processing subscription payments (coming soon)

These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Cookies

CarsInStock uses cookies that are essential to the operation of the platform, including session cookies for authentication and Cloudflare security cookies. Google Analytics also uses cookies to measure site traffic. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Data Retention

We retain your account and listing data for as long as your account is active. Vehicle listings automatically expire after 7 days to ensure fresh inventory. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure password hashing, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us. You can also delete your account through the platform settings.

Children's Privacy

CarsInStock is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email. Your continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@carsinstock.com.
