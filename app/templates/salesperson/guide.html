The one idea behind all of it

Your cars come off the site automatically after 7 days.

That's the whole point. When a customer sees a car on your page, it's actually there. No ghost listings, no wasted trips, no “sorry, that sold three weeks ago.”

It also means the site only works if you keep it fed. A car you don't renew disappears. That's not a bug — that's the promise.

Pro Social Ad

Makes a finished social media ad for any car in your inventory — photo, price, your name, your store's Google rating.

Tap it, pick a car, pick a template, you get an image. Post it to Facebook, your neighborhood groups, marketplace, wherever you already are.

Eight seconds. No design work, nothing to write.

Storefront Backdrop

Puts a professional background behind your Top Pick car on your storefront page.

Tap to choose a different one. Purely visual — it makes your featured car look like an ad instead of a lot photo.

Quick Share

Getting your store link in front of people, fast.

  • Copy link — your storefront address, ready to paste
  • Digital card — your business card as a web page
  • Text template — a pre-written message you can send a customer
  • Weekly post — a ready-made post for social
  • Send My Card via Text — texts your card straight to someone's phone

Use these buttons rather than copying the address out of the browser bar. The buttons add a tag that tells us the visit came from you and which channel it came from. Copy the URL by hand and that tracking is lost — your work stops showing up in your numbers.

Pro Mailer Generator

Direct mail to the homes around your sale, each letter carrying a $500 promotional marketing savings offer good for 30 days.

Neighbor letters — you sold a car, this mails the homes around the buyer. Congratulates the neighborhood, makes the offer.

Reference letters — your buyer listed someone as a personal reference. This writes to that person by name and mentions the friend who bought. Warmer than a cold letter; someone they know just bought from you.

When a customer walks in holding one: the offer has to be presented to you directly, at the time of purchase.

Your Bird Dog Network

Powered by MyCarReferral. People send you buyers; you sell, and you send them a gift.

  • Send via Text — get your referral link to someone
  • My link — your referral address
  • Show QR — your referral code as a QR someone can scan on the spot
  • Manage — see who's sent you what
  • How it works — the full explanation

The counters at the top show how many referrals are pending and how many closed.

My Store

  • View my storefront — your public page. This is what customers see.
  • Storefront backdrop — same as above
  • My vehicles — how many cars you have live right now
  • My leads — people who tapped I'm Interested on your page. This and My vehicles both open the same inventory page.

Check leads first thing. They came looking at a specific car, on your page, and asked for you by name. That is the warmest call you will make all day. Call them fast.

My Vehicles

Adding a car

+ Add a Vehicle. Details, photos, save.

Your manager reviews and approves it before it goes live, so it won't appear on your storefront the instant you hit save.

The 7-day clock starts when you submit, not when it's approved. A car that sits two days waiting for approval goes live with five days left. If something's been pending a while, tell your manager.

The 7-day clock

Every car shows how many days it has left. At zero it comes off your page automatically.

Check this daily. Ten seconds, and it's the difference between a full page and an empty one. Renewing is one tap.

The gold star — your Top Pick

Tap the star on any car and it becomes the featured vehicle at the top of your storefront, with your name on it: “JOE'S TOP PICK THIS WEEK.”

Only one car can be your Top Pick at a time. Tapping the star on a different one moves it.

Use it on whatever you most want to move this week — the aged unit, the one with the best story, the one you'd walk a customer to first.

Pencil and trash

Pencil edits the car — price, mileage, description. Trash removes it.

Sorting

$ Low→High and $ High→Low reorder your list by price.

A daily routine that takes five minutes

  1. Leads first. Anything new, call it.
  2. Renew anything close to expiring.
  3. Post new arrivals.
  4. Move the star to whatever you want to push today.
  5. Share one car — use the Quick Share buttons, not a copied link.

Before the lot gets busy. Every morning.

If something looks wrong

Tell your manager. Don't work around it and don't assume it's you — if a button doesn't do what you expect, that's worth knowing about.

Note what you tapped and what happened. That's usually enough to get it fixed the same day.
