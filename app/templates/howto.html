Your CarsInStock Playbook

Everything you need to build your brand, move inventory, and turn clicks into customers.

1
Set Up Your Storefront
Your storefront is your digital dealership. It's the first thing customers see, so make it count. A complete profile builds trust and gets people reaching out.

Here's how:

  1. Click My Profile in the top menu
  2. Add your display name (this becomes your storefront URL)
  3. Write a short, punchy bio — tell customers why they should work with you
  4. Add your phone number and email so customers can reach you directly
  5. Hit Save and you're live!
Pro Tip: Your storefront URL is yours to share everywhere — text it, post it on social media, put it in your email signature. It's your personal brand.
2
Add & Manage Your Vehicles
Fresh inventory is king. Every car you add goes live on your storefront instantly. Listings stay active for 7 days to keep your page fresh — no ghost cars here.

Here's how:

  1. Click + Add Vehicle in the top menu
  2. Enter the year, make, model, trim, price, and mileage
  3. Upload a clean photo — first impressions matter
  4. Add color, transmission, and any extras
  5. Hit Save and it's live on your storefront
Pro Tip: Listings expire after 7 days automatically. This keeps your page looking active and current. Re-add a car anytime to refresh it.
3
Use the VIN Scanner
Skip the typing. The VIN scanner reads the barcode or text on any vehicle and auto-fills the year, make, model, and trim. Walk the lot, scan, and list — that fast.

Here's how:

  1. Go to + Add Vehicle
  2. Click Scan VIN at the top of the form
  3. Point your phone camera at the VIN barcode or number on the windshield
  4. The system reads it and fills in the vehicle details automatically
  5. Review, add price and photos, then save
Pro Tip: Walk the lot before your shift starts. Scan 5-10 cars in minutes. By the time the doors open, your storefront is stocked and ready.
4
Manage Customers & Send Emails
Every lead that clicks "I'm Interested" becomes a customer in your database. Stay in touch, follow up, and close deals — all from one place.

Here's how:

  1. Click My Customers to see everyone who's reached out
  2. View their name, email, phone, and which vehicle they liked
  3. Click Send Email to follow up directly
  4. Add new customers manually if you meet them in person
  5. Keep your list growing — these are YOUR customers, not the dealership's
Pro Tip: Follow up within 5 minutes of getting a lead. Speed wins deals. A quick "Hey, I saw you liked the Charger — want to come see it?" goes a long way.
5
Share Your Page & Get Leads
Your storefront only works if people see it. The more you share, the more leads you get. Treat your URL like your business card — hand it out everywhere.

Here's how:

  1. Copy your storefront link from My Storefront
  2. Text it to customers who ask "what do you have on the lot?"
  3. Post it on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok — wherever your buyers are
  4. Add it to your email signature and business cards
  5. Share individual vehicles using the Share button on each listing
Pro Tip: Post a new vehicle on social media every morning with your storefront link. Consistency builds your following and drives traffic to your page.
