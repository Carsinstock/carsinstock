About CarsInStock

Born from the Showroom Floor

CarsInStock wasn't built in a boardroom. It was built from over twenty-five years of managing a car dealership — watching the industry evolve, adapting to every shift, and witnessing firsthand what happens when talented salespeople lose control of their own careers.

The car business has always been one of the greatest industries in the world. It rewards hustle, rewards personality, and rewards the kind of relentless work ethic that most people only talk about. A true car salesperson doesn't just sell vehicles — they build relationships, solve problems, and create financial lives that most nine-to-five careers simply cannot offer.

But something changed.

The BDC Era

Business Development Centers were introduced to streamline the sales process, and in many ways they did. But they also created something nobody planned for: a generation of order takers. Salespeople who once thrived on their own drive began waiting — waiting for the next lead to land in their inbox, waiting for the dealership's advertising to deliver a customer to their desk.

Pay plans shifted. They stopped rewarding volume and started rewarding just enough. The hunger faded. The independence disappeared. And the very thing that made this career extraordinary — the ability to control your own income through your own effort — started slipping away.

We Built the Way Back

CarsInStock exists because we believe in the car salesperson. Not the order taker. The salesperson.

This platform gives you something no dealership BDC ever will: your own digital storefront. Your name. Your inventory. Your customers. A professional, branded presence online that works for you around the clock — capturing leads, showcasing your vehicles, and putting you back in the driver's seat of your own career.

No more relying solely on dealership advertising. No more hoping the BDC sends a good lead your way. CarsInStock is the tool that lets you take initiative, build your own book of business, and create the kind of financial success this industry has always been capable of delivering.

Your Roadmap. Your Success.

The car business isn't dying — it's waiting for salespeople who are ready to take it back. CarsInStock gives you the technology, the platform, and the freedom to do exactly that.

Every car salesperson works hard every single day. Now there's a platform that works just as hard for you.
